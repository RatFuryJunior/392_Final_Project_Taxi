b)
I would argue that for this question there are semi discernable clusters.


There is one seemingly noisy cluster (Short distance, 30sd day away travel time)


One cluster that is for people who travel sort distances dont tip much and travel for short amounts of time. This is seemingly in city travelers hopping from block to block.


Then one cluster of much longer distance as well as longer time, with a slight increase in tipping, I would consider these to be commuters traveling across the city


There are seemingly no better clusters to create. Also note that clusters are sillouette scored based on the most recent 5000 records not the entire dataset. N_Clusters was also decided via the silloutte score determined here.
